Our Mission

INtERSPECT TRAINING SERVICES believes in human greatness and potential, thus, it aims to help people achieve life goals through effective and high-impact training, coaching, counseling and prevent maladjustment through proper mental health care programs.

Our Vision

INtERSPECT TRAINING SERVICES envisions to develop an empowered and caring community members regardless of their demographic background; and decrease, if not eliminate, the mental health related disorders leading to an emotionally, psychologically, spiritually and physically balanced individuals and community.
